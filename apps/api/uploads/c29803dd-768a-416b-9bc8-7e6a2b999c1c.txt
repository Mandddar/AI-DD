ERROR 1 — CHANGE OF CONTROL CLAUSE
Two (2) customer framework agreements (Klinikum Süd, value
€1.8M/year; and BioVita AG, value €1.2M/year) contain change-of-

before any change in the ownership structure exceeding 50%.

Neither customer has been approached regarding consent.

All other contracts are freely assignable or contain standard

reviewed by external counsel (Freshfields) in Q2 2024.

ERROR 2 — TRANSFER PRICING DOCUMENTATION GAP
The company pays a management fee of €380,000 per year to its
parent entity, MediTech Holding BV (Netherlands), for "group
services." No transfer pricing documentation (Verrechnungs-
preisdokumentation) exists to substantiate the arm's-length
nature of this fee. Under §1 AStG, this creates a risk of
adjustment by the German tax authorities in a future audit.

Estimated tax risk: €95,000–€190,000 (depending on audit scope
and reassessment methodology).
▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓▓

VAT is handled correctly. Input tax deduction is documented.
